About Company Page


Election Management Software is one of the top Political Consulting Firms in India. The need for accurate analysis in today’s world is as important as the information gathered through that analysis. A combination of these two gives rise to a powerful methodology which assesses the current scenario and helps in decision making; optimizing the available resources for gaining the best possible returns. We, at Electionmanagement, are in the business of gathering valuable and accurate information, then analyzing it to generate political insights. For this purpose, we employ innovative and cutting-edge tools. At present, we are using the expertise of our staff and technology alike to help elected representatives manage their constituencies in an organized manner.


Our management team consists of the alumnus of topmost educational institutes such as IIT, IIM and NIT. The IT and database management department is headed by engineers and MCAs from prestigious institutes. Experienced social sciences post-graduates look after political, social and economic research division and graduates from leading institutes of Mass Communication and Political Science work as surveyors and data collectors.
